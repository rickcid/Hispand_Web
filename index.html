About Us

Hispand Consulting was born out of the need for guidance and experience that businesses require when attempting to expand globally. We are a group of energetic individuals with extensive experience in developing global operations. We use our global expertise and network to successfully develop your project.

We use insight from our experiences in global marketing, finance, and business intelligence to develop your operation and help you gain a competitive edge in the market.
